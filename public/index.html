About us

KW70 Fix is a leading real estate investment firm in the World, headquartered in New York with offices in London, Chicago, Barcelona, Vienna, Rome, Las Vegas.

KW70 Fix initiative seeks out regions and markets with robust underlying catalysts, such as burgeoning technology sectors, a diverse workforce, and a strong entrepreneurial culture. Once identified, we aim to bring our extensive knowledge and resources to these carefully chosen areas, partnering with local stakeholders to create a thriving physical environment that promotes growth and prosperity. In those carefully selected markets and locations, we bring capital and expertise to create or enhance the physical built environment that can transform communities.
